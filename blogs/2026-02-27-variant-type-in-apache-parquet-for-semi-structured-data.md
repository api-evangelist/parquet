The Apache Parquet community is excited to announce the addition of the Variant type —a feature that brings native support for semi-structured data to Parquet, significantly improving efficiency compared to less efficient formats such as JSON. This marks a significant addition to Parquet, demonstrating how the format continues to evolve to meet modern data engineering needs.
